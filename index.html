Features

Focusing on cutting-edge technologies in AI, integrating these advanced fields into the curriculum, research, and industry collaborations. This makes sure that students are well-prepared for the future of technology and innovation.

  • Our Department

    The Department offers a 4-year B.Tech in AI and Machine Learning, specializing in algorithm design and data science to equip students for careers in industry and research.

  • Our Vision

    To become an integrative department through creativity and innovation in developing systems with Artificial Intelligence and Data Science to serve society.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to develop future-ready talent through industry collaboration and practical experiences, while upholding the highest standards of ethics and innovation.
